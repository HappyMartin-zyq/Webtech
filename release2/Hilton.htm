Hilton Hotels & Resorts

Hilton Hotels & Resorts is Hilton's flagship brand and one of the largest hotel brands in the world.[7] The brand is targeted at both business and leisure travelers with locations in major city centers, near airports, convention centers, and popular vacation destinations around the world.[8] Hilton Hotels & Resorts participates in Hilton Honors, Hilton's guest loyalty program. Members who book directly through Hilton-owned channels receive exclusive discounts and amenities such as free Wi-Fi, digital check-in, keyless entry, and the ability to use a mobile app to choose specific rooms.[9][10] Type Subsidiary Industry Hospitality Founded May 31, 1919; 101 years ago (as Hilton Hotels) Founder Conrad Nicholson Hilton Headquarters McLean, Virginia, U.S. Number of locations 584 hotels (December 31, 2019)[1][2] Area served Worldwide Parent Hilton Worldwide Website hilton.com
